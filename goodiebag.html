Goodie Bag

Overview

Goodie bag is an application that allows users to shop for groceries and have them delivered.

Objective

Grocery shopping should be easy and accessible to everyone, and I kept all of this in mind when designing Goodie Bag. I set goals for what I wanted to accomplish:

  • Easily readable text throughout the entire application
  • Photos are all similar in style
  • Very simple in layout and design

Purpose & Context

This is my 4th and last student project. I chose the "online store" brief that was given, and Goodie Bag is what I came up with.
